Ushaka Warriors hammered opponents.


Saturday afternoon held an exciting fixture for Ushaka Warriors. On the 25th of June 2022, after a two week break saw Ushaka Warriors hosts to Amabhubesi. The game was set to kick off at 15:30pm at the Mandini Country Club. Throughout the first half of the match both teams were fierce in taking the advantage before half time. The visitors were victories in doing so as they led by 3 points, 14-17. As the second half was underway, both teams fought hard but there could only be one winner in the end. Ushaka Warriors won the encounter 47-17 to claim the win and bonus point, which takes the team up the ladder to the second position on the table of the DRSU CF GLEW 5th Division. Head coach for Ushaka Warriors, Bukhosi Mndaweni told iLembe Tribune, “Going forward if the team continues to bind and work together with the same energy and drive, we can remain unstoppable in our league. As management we are impressed with how the youngsters are performing this season.” Man of the match for the Forwards saw a surprising tie with the likes of Tau Mthethwa and Qiniso Masuku. While the man of the match for the backline, who has been the top performer throughout this season Tony Chivamo. Ushaka Warriors will play the Reservoir Hills Rhinos on the 09th of July 2022 at home. This encounter is set to be a tough fixture thus far in the 2022 competition for Ushaka Warriors
